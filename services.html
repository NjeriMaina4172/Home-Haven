Our Services

Medical Services

24hrs Emergency

At Home Haven, we provide top-notch medical care for you and your loved ones in times of emergency. Our board certified emergency physicians, along with dedicated nurses, will stabilize your condition and guide you on the path to recovery. Our emergency department operates 24/7, ensuring you receive safe and efficient medical attention. Should you need further treatment, we will coordinate with other healthcare providers, including telemedicine specialists and transfer you to a hospital if necessary..

Consultations

Our doctors are proud to offer both home visit consultations and telemedicine consultations, so you can receive the care you need no matter where you are. With home visits, our doctor will come directly to your door, providing you with in-person attention. For those who prefer the ease and flexibility of virtual consultations, our telemedicine services allow you to connect with a doctor via video call. Say goodbye to long wait times, crowded waiting rooms, and the hassle of traveling to and from appointments. Let us bring top-notch medical care directly to you,

Laboratory & Radiology

we offer top-notch laboratory and radiology services. Our professionals use state-of-the-art equipment to deliver accurate and reliable results quickly. Whether you need routine blood work or more complex imaging services, we've got you covered. With our in-home services, you can avoid the hassle of traveling to a clinic and can instead receive the care you need in a comfortable and familiar environment. Plus, our services are fully integrated with your electronic health records, ensuring a seamless experience from start to finish.

Nutritional Services

Our nutritionists will patiently work with you to develop a customized meal plan that meets your unique needs, taking into account your health goals, dietary restrictions, workout plans and lifestyle. Whether you're looking to lose weight, manage a chronic condition, or simply improve your overall health, our nutrition services will provide you with the tools and support you need to achieve your goals. We believe that good nutrition is the foundation of good health, and our goal is to empower you to make the best choices for your well-being. With convenient in-home consultations, healthy meal delivery options, and ongoing support from our team of experts, our nutritional services are designed to make it easy for you to achieve and maintain a healthy, balanced diet. Contact us today to schedule your first consultation and start your journey to a healthier, happier you!

Psychiatry and Behavioral Health

We offer comprehensive psychiatry and behavioral health services to help you achieve your best possible mental and emotional well-being. Our psychiatrists and behavioral health specialists are dedicated to providing you with the care and support you need to manage a wide range of mental health concerns, including anxiety, depression, mood disorders, and more. Whether you're looking to improve your mental health, manage symptoms of a condition, or simply find a safe and supportive space to work through life's challenges, our psychiatry and behavioral health services are here to help. We believe in a holistic approach to mental health, and our treatments are tailored to meet your unique needs and goals. Contact us today to schedule a consultation and start your journey towards a brighter tomorrow.

Therapies

Our team of experienced therapists offers a variety of therapies:
-physical therapy to help you recover from injuries, manage chronic conditions, and improve your overall physical function.
-pediatric therapy services designed to support children with developmental, behavioral, and learning difficulties.
-speech and language therapy services aimed at helping individuals overcome speech impediments and improve their communication skills.
-Our occupational therapy services help individuals perform their daily activities more effectively and independently.
-massage therapy to relieve stress and muscle pain, acupuncture to alleviate a variety of physical and emotional conditions,
-genetic counseling to help you understand the potential risks and benefits of genetic testing
