Frequently Asked Questions

What is Home Haven?+

A web application provides in-home health services to individuals who require medical assistance in the comfort of their own home. It offers a range of services from telemedicine consultations to medication deliveries.

How does the service work? +

Create an account on the web application's website Request the services you need through the website Receive a personalized treatment plan from a team of healthcare professionals Schedule appointments and receive medical attention in the comfort of your own home Make payments for the services received through the website's secure payment portal.

Are heaalthcare proffesionals Qualified? +

Yes, all healthcare professionals working through the web application are licensed and board-certified in their respective fields.

How do i pay for the service? +

Payments for the services provided through the web application are made online through the website's secure payment portal. The available options for payment may include credit/debit cards, online bank transfers, and other secure online payment methods. Please check the website for the most up-to-date information on available payment methods.

Who can use the service? +

The services are available to anyone who needs medical attention and would prefer if they received it in familiar surroundings, regardless of their age or medical condition. This includes patients of all ages and medical conditions who are in private homes, skilled nursing facilities, or assisted living settings. The services aim to make medical care accessible and convenient for anyone who needs it.
